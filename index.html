MY FAVORITE CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIE RECIPE

Read on for a very simple, yet delicious, chocolate chip cookie recipe.

This recipe for yummy chocolate chip cookies only needs 6 ingredients!

  • Butter: 1 stick, softened salted or unsalted
  • Sugar: 3/4 cup light or dark brown sugar
  • Flour: 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Egg: 1 large
  • Salt: 1/4 tsp
  • And of course....chocolate chips: 1 cup

Ready for the cooking directions?

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, cream togther the butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add the egg to the butter/sugar mixture and mix until incorporated.
  4. Stir in the flour, salt, and chocolate chips just until combined.
  5. Scoop into mounds about 2 TBSP each onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees F until just set on the edges and middle still looks a little bit undercooked. About 8-12 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool on the baking sheets for 5 minutes before removing.
